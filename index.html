The astronaut needs to return to her spaceship

Slide the astronaut and the robosatellites to make them float up, right, left, or down. They can only float in a direction if someone is in the way and can catch them.

Reload to get a new set of puzzles!

This game was built by IMAGINARY, a traveling exhibition that shows mathematics can be beautiful, interactive, something you can touch, or a way to explore remote galaxies with your mind. It has visited over 66 countries since 2007.
